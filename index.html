Though their name may suggest otherwise, The Wombats are not in fact from Australia. They're an English indie rock band that formed in Liverpool in 2003.
Their three-man lineup has never changed over their two decades of playing. Frontman Matthew Murphy commands the group with his sincere, belting delivery and driving guitar play. The Wombats have made a reputation for themselves with their knack for sticky, anthemic hooks paired with creative lyrics exploring the comedy and tragedy of trying (and mostly failing) to find meaningful connection.
